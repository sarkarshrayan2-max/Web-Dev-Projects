# ⏱️ Timing Precision Game

An addictive, arcade-style web game that tests your reflexes and consistency. Stop the rapidly moving indicator exactly inside the target zone to survive and build your score!

## 🎮 How to Play

- **Goal:** Stop the moving slider exactly inside the highlighted target area.
- **Controls:** Press `SPACEBAR` on Desktop, or simply **Tap the screen** on Mobile/Tablet devices.
- **Scoring:** 
  - **Perfect Hit** (dead center): High points, builds your streak multiplier faster.
  - **Good Hit** (inside zone): Base points, maintains your streak.
  - **Miss** (outside zone): 0 points, breaks your streak, and you lose 1 Life.
- **Difficulty:** As your streak grows, the speed of the slider increases. The target area also randomly shifts to a new position after every hit.
- **Game Over:** You start with 3 Lives. The game ends when you miss 3 times.

## ✨ Features

- **Endless Survival Mode:** Keep playing to beat your high score.
- **Streak Multiplier System:** Rewards consistency and perfect timing.
- **Local Leaderboards:** Saves your top 10 high scores directly in the browser.
- **Visual Feedback:** Pulse animations and screen shakes provide immediate feedback on your performance.
- **Fully Responsive:** Playable anywhere!

## 🛠️ Built With

- **HTML5:** Semantic architecture.
- **CSS3:** Custom variables, grid/flex layouts, and CSS animations.
- **Vanilla JavaScript:** Utilizes `requestAnimationFrame` for a butter-smooth 60fps game loop. No external libraries.

## 🚀 How to Run

1. Navigate to the `Projects/Timing Precision Game` directory.
2. Open `index.html` in your favorite web browser.
3. Start tapping!
